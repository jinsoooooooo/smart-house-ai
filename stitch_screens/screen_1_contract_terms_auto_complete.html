Special Agreement Terms

Article 1 [Deposit & Rent]

The lessee shall pay the deposit of ₩500,000,000 (KRW) upon signing this contract. The remainder shall be paid upon moving in.

Article 2 [Maintenance Obligations]

  1. The lessor guarantees the facility against structural water leakage for a period of 6 months from the move-in date.
  2. The lessee is responsible for minor repairs (e.g., light bulb replacement, battery changes for door locks) costing under ₩50,000.
  3. If mold occurs due to the lessee's negligence (lack of ventilation), restoration costs shall be borne by the lessee.

Article 3 [Prohibitions]

Raising pets is strictly prohibited without prior written consent. If violated, the lessee must pay for professional cleaning and deodorizing services upon vacating.
